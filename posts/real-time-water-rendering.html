Real-Time Water Rendering

Instructor: Jon Sánchez

Languages: C++

Tools: OpenGL, GLFW

Research: Real-Time Water Rendering - Paper

Release: GitHub

Overview:

As a final project for the real-time rendering course, I decided to research about the techinques for rendering water dynamics. I decided then to implement a water tool that come in handy in most situations without assuming the size of the medium we want to render. Allowing to render realistic ambiences to oceans, rapids, lakes or fountains.

My implementation combines the performance of the global motion and the flexibility of the localise flow to be able to simulate highly interactive water. However, I also discover that hardware and algorithms are recently evolving into particle simulations which will add the next step into water realism whenever its performance will be manageable in real-time gaming.

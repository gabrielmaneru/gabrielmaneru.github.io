Soul Dancer

High Concept: Soul Dancer is a 2D top-down fast-paced rhythm-based strategic game. The hero of our story is a long-dead Mexican warrior resurrected by the spirit of music to find his way back to life. Follow his progress through the different levels facing enemies and upgrading his equipment!

Instructor: Thomas Komair

Engine: Custom C++ monolithic engine

Language: C++

Release: DigiPen Gallery

Overview: Soul Dancer was my second game developed during the spring of my freshman year. I was the first game developed from scratch in C++.

Role: Technical Lead

I developed a random-based dungeon grid generator and implemented it involving rendering, collisions, movement and enemy AI. Once I finish with the engine part, I worked on multiple areas such as UI or menu design.

Gravity Panda

High Concept: Gravity Panda is a fast-paced 2D platformer where you play as a hungry panda who uses his power to control gravity to reach and eat bamboo!

Instructor: Thomas Komair

Engine: Zero Engine

Language: Zilchscript (C# based)

Release: DigiPen Gallery

Overview: Gravity Panda is the first project I worked on at Digipen during the first semester of my Freshmen year. It took 3 months of development using the in house engine. The team was composed of 3 programmers.

Role: Technical Lead

I programmed most of the main mechanics of the player and the hazards. I also designed and created the menus as well as developing some levels.
